Travels Abroad

Shortly after completing my undergraduate degree, I traveled to the UK. BUNAC provides work visas for American students to work in the UK. While working in London I met coworkers and other BUNAC attendees. I have listed a few of the places I traveled to. }

  • Nottingham, England

    I spent Christmas in Nottingham with a family that was orignally from Nottingham but was stationed in Norfolk, VA; my hometown. The family met my parents at a parade and my mother shared with them that I was in London and would not be home for Christmas. They insisted I travel to their home in Nottingham to have a traditional English Christmas.

  • Paris, France

    Two of my friends from High school traveled to London and we took the Eurostar to Paris. We spent 3 days in the city and traveled to must see sites such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre. We ate our weight in baguettes and crepes!

  • Prague, Czech Republic

    • I travled with a group of friends from London to Prague. We visited a few night clubs and drank absinthe - do not recommend! We toured the Prague Castle, Charles Bridge and the Astronomical Clock.

  • Corfu, Greece

    • My roommates and I traveled to Greece after our work visas expired. This was our last trip together before returning to the US. We spent most of our days on the beach and shopping.

For more information about the BUNAC program, you can visit their webpage : https://bunac.org/
