Travels Abroad

BUNAC

BUNAC provides work visas for American students to work in the UK. Shortly after completing my undergraduate degree, I enrolled in the program and travled to the UK, otherwise known as a GAP year for some.

I have listed a few of the places I traveled to:

  • Nottingham, England
  • Paris, France
  • Corfu, Greece

Nottingham

I spent Christmas in Nottingham with a family that was orignally from Nottingham but was stationed in Norfolk, VA; my hometown. I was exposed to a traditional English Christmas.

Paris

Two of my friends from High school traveled to London and we took the Eurostar to Paris. We spent 3 days in the city and traveled to must see sites such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre. We ate our weight in baguettes and crepes!

Corfu

My roommates and I traveled to Greece after our work visas expired. This was our last trip together before returning to the US. We spent most of our days on the beach and shopping.

For more information about the BUNAC program, you can visit their webpage : https://bunac.org/
